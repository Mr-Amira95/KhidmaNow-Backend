04 Clear Space & Misuse

Give the mark room to breathe.

Maintain a minimum clear space of X on all sides — equal to the height of the wordmark. Never crowd the logo with text, imagery, or borders.

XXXX

Sizing rules

Minimum digital width: 96 px (full lockup), 32 px (icon only).

Minimum print width: 25 mm (full lockup), 8 mm (icon only).

Alignment: Always reproduce from approved master files. Never recreate or trace the logo.

File formats: SVG for digital, PDF/EPS for print, PNG with transparency as a fallback.

Don'ts

Avoid the following misuses to preserve brand integrity.

Don't stretch or distort
Don't recolor outside palette
Don't add harsh shadows
Don't rotate or tilt
Don't place on busy backgrounds
Always use master files
05 Color Palette

A confident, action-ready system.

Blue grounds the brand in trust. Orange drives action and urgency. Green confirms completion. Together they form a balanced, accessible palette.

Usage proportions

Use a 60 / 30 / 10 ratio across most surfaces — blue for structure and trust, orange for action and emphasis, green for confirmation.

Reserve green for success states, completion indicators, and positive confirmations only.

07 Tone of Voice

Clear, warm, and efficient.

We write the way we serve — quickly, plainly, and with respect for the reader's time.

Do

"Your provider is on the way — arriving in 12 minutes."

"Tap to confirm and we'll handle the rest."

"Service completed. Rate your experience."

Don't

"Please be advised that your service request has been allocated."

"Kindly proceed by clicking the aforementioned button."

"Thank you for utilizing our esteemed platform."
